Gujhiya

  • Heat 2 tbsp ghee (clarified butter) in a small pan or bowl.
  • Pour the ghee on the flours.
  • Rub the ghee with the flours, with your fingertips to form a bread crumb like texture.
  • Knead the dough till firm.
  • Cover with a moist cloth and keep aside for 30 minutes.
  • Stir the khoya continuously on low heat.
